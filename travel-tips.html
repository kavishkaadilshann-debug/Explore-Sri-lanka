Travel Tips & Guide

Essential tips, local advice, and resources for an unforgettable journey

📌 Frequently Asked Questions

Sri Lanka has two monsoons. December to April is ideal for southern/western beaches and the central highlands. From May to September, eastern beaches (like Trincomalee) are sunny and perfect.
Scenic trains (like Kandy to Ella) are a must-book. For cities, budget-friendly Tuk-Tuks are popular. Uber and PickMe apps work wonderfully in Colombo and major towns to get fixed prices.
Traditional Rice and Curry, Kottu Roti, and Hoppers can be spicy! Don't hesitate to ask restaurants for "mild spice" if you are sensitive. Drink fresh King Coconut water (Thambili) to cool down.
When visiting sacred Buddhist temples or Hindu Kovils, dress modestly. You must cover your shoulders and knees. Hats and shoes must also be removed before entering temple compounds.
You can purchase tourist SIM cards directly at Colombo Airport (BIA) arrival hall. Providers like Dialog and Mobitel offer cheap high-speed data packages (typically $10 USD for 20GB+).
